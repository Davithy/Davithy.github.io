Movie Poster

Tool(s): Clip Studio Paint, Adobe Illustrator

Description: This piece comes from a short story of the same name posted by Professor Floor. I then thumbnail ideas on Clip Studio Paint, my go-to digital art app. After putting down at least 4 ideas, I take the attributes I like best from each of them and translate them into shapes and patterns in Adobe Illustrator. For the paper stacks, I used an asset from the Adobe library, but used the pen tool for about everything else.

Movie Poster for the Nine Billion Names of God.
