Glitch Art

Tool(s): Notepad++, Audacity, Adobe Illustrator

Description: I took the original image of an Azure tit, a small bird, and processed it through Notepad++, editing the metadata of the file while maintaining its stability. Afterwards, I took the remaining file and ran it through Audacity, an audio editing app. I cut up some of the wav-form, saved the file, and finally made the circular pattern with Adobe Illustrator.
